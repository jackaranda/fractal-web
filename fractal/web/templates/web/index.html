FRACTAL

Future Resilience for African Cities and Lands

FRACTAL is one of the Future Climate For Africa (FCFA) regional consortia that are currently being launched. Led by the Climate System Analysis Group (CSAG) at the University of Cape Town, the FRACTAL project is focussed specifically on climate risks and decision making in cities within their regional dependencies.

The projectâ€™s overarching aim is to advance scientific knowledge about regional climate responses to anthropogenic forcings, and to enhance the integration of this knowledge into decision making at the co-dependent city-region scale that responsibly contributes to resilient development pathways. This is addressed through an iterative, transdisciplinary co-exploration / co-production approach, worked through a set of deep-focus case studies.
